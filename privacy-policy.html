Privacy Policy for BNTF

Last updated: September 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Bristow Norge Teknisk Forening (BNTF) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the BNTF mobile application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The BNTF App is designed to operate entirely on your device without collecting or transmitting personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal information is requested or collected
  • No usage data or analytics are collected
  • No location data is accessed or stored
  • No device identifiers are collected
  • No crash reports or diagnostic data are sent to us

3. Local Data Processing

All app functionality occurs locally on your device:

  • Calculation Tools: ACTT calculations, torque conversions, and weight conversions are performed locally on your device
  • App Preferences: Any settings or preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS standard UserDefaults
  • Temporary Data: Input values in calculators are temporarily stored in device memory only while the app is active

4. External Links

The App contains links to external websites and resources. These external sites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted:

  • There is no risk of personal data breaches from our app
  • All calculations and data remain on your device
  • Your privacy is protected by design

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

No information is shared with third parties, partners, or service providers.

7. Children's Privacy

The BNTF App is designed for professional use by aircraft maintenance personnel and is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data protection rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) are not applicable. However, you can:

  • Delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • Clear app data through iOS settings
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

10. International Data Transfers

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfers associated with the use of this app.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), this privacy policy serves as notice that no personal data processing occurs through the use of this app.
