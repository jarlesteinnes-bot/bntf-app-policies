Q: The app crashes when I open it

A: Try these steps in order:

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your iPhone/iPad
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • If the problem persists, contact support with your device model and iOS version

Q: My calculations seem incorrect

A: Please note:

  • All calculation tools are provided as reference aids only
  • Always verify results independently
  • Follow official maintenance procedures and regulations
  • Contact support if you believe there's a calculation error

Q: The app is running slowly

A: Try these solutions:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have adequate storage space
  • Update to the latest iOS version

Q: How do I use the ACTT Calculator?

A: The Aircraft Completion Time Calculator:

  • Enter your current ACTT hours and minutes
  • Add individual flight times (up to 6 flights)
  • The app automatically calculates your new ACTT
  • Always verify results with official documentation

Q: How accurate are the conversion tools?

A: Our conversion tools:

  • Use standard industry conversion factors
  • Are updated regularly for accuracy
  • Should always be cross-checked with official specifications
  • Are intended for reference purposes only

Q: Can I use this app offline?

A: Yes! The BNTF app works completely offline:

  • All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • No internet connection required for core functions
  • External links require internet connectivity
